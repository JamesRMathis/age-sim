Can you make it?

Everyone dies. This is a fact known by most people. Many also wonder: "When will I die?" While it's nearly impossible to say exactly, we can use statistics to get a rough approximation! Specifically, we use statistics collected by the US Social Security Agency to find the chance of dying every year. Simply enter the age that you would like to reach (between 0 and 119) and your biological sex (the one on your birth certificate) and we'll simulate between 100k and 300k lifetimes of the average person per second, depending on your machine. Then you'll see exactly how many simulations reached your desired age!
